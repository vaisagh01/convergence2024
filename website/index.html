DEPARTMENT OF MATHEMATICS

Fest

The Department of Mathematics provides opportunities for students to appreciate Mathematics, exhibit their talents and demonstrate their Mathematical knowledge outside the boundaries of the classrooms. Convergence is one such stage which aims to inspire and encourage budding mathematicians by providing them a platform for exchange of thoughts and ideas through various competitive events. Since its inception, convergence has aimed to help students explore Mathematics outside the boundaries of classrooms in an interactive and entertaining manner.
